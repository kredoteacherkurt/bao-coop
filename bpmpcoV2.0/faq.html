Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about joining and transacting with Baao Coop.

How do I become a member?

To become a member, you must first attend the Pre-Membership Education Seminar (PMES). Afterward, submit the required documents (IDs, pictures, Birth Certificate/Marriage Contract) and pay the initial fees totaling Php 900.00. You can start by downloading the forms online or visiting our nearest branch.

Can non-residents of Baao join the cooperative?

Yes! While we originated in Baao, our cooperative is open to individuals residing in areas where we have satellite branches, including Goa, Iriga City, Ocampo, Naga City, Sipocot, and Polangui.

What are the requirements to apply for a loan?

Members must be in good standing with updated share capital. General requirements include a completed loan application form, valid IDs, proof of income (payslips or business permits), and depending on the loan amount, a co-maker or collateral may be required.

What is the Damayang Pangkalusugan (DPK)?

The DPK is a health and medical assistance program exclusively for members. It acts as an emergency health fund that members can tap into during hospitalizations or severe medical situations.

How can I view my account balance?

You can view your savings and loan balances by bringing your passbook to any of our branches for updating. You can also view your transaction history by logging into the Member Portal via this website.
