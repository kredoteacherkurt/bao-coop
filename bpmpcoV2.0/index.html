Our Legacy & Mission

Founded with a vision of mutual help and economic empowerment, the Baao Parish Multi-Purpose Cooperative has grown from a small savings group into a cornerstone of our community's financial stability.

Our mission is to provide accessible financial services while fostering a culture of thrift, cooperation, and social responsibility among our members.
