- Who We Are -

Rooted in Faith, Built Through Cooperation

Baao Parish Multi-Purpose Cooperative (BPMPCO) is a member-owned, community-driven institution grounded in cooperative principles and guided by shared faith and responsibility. Established through the collective effort of parishioners and community members, it exists to uplift lives by providing accessible financial services and sustainable livelihood support.

More than a financial institution, BPMPCO is a platform for mutual growth—where members are not just clients, but active participants in building a stronger, more resilient community. It continues to evolve while staying anchored on its core purpose: service through unity and cooperation.

- Our Path -

A Journey of Growth, Service, and Cooperative Strength

1963

Foundation

Registered as Baao Parish Cooperative Credit Union, Inc. (BPCCUI) on August 26, 1963, starting with 50 members and Php 1,193.00 in capital. Founded by parishioners led by the Hijas de Maria, with support from the Baao Parish Council, to provide accessible credit to underserved members of the community.

1989

Milestone Growth

Reached Php 1 million in total assets and was recognized as a “Millionaire Cooperative,” marking a significant step in its financial stability and growth.

1991

Formal Registration

Re-registered with the Cooperative Development Authority under Republic Act No. 6938 as Baao Parish Multi-Purpose Cooperative (BPMPCO), expanding its legal and operational scope.

1990s–2000s

Expansion of Services

Transitioned from a credit union into a multi-purpose cooperative, introducing savings, expanded loan products, and livelihood programs to better serve members’ growing needs.

2010s–Present

Regional Expansion

Expanded its reach across the Bicol region, establishing branches in Goa, Iriga City, Ocampo, Naga City, Sipocot, and Polangui, strengthening financial inclusion and accessibility.

2022

Recognition

Honored at the Asia’s Pinnacle Awards as the “Most Dedicated & Exemplary Trusted Cooperative in Camarines Sur,” recognizing its strong performance and leadership.

Today

Continuing the Mission

BPMPCO continues to uphold cooperative values while delivering inclusive financial services, empowering members, and strengthening communities for long-term development.
