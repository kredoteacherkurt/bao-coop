Data Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Baao Parish Multi-Purpose Cooperative (BPMPCO) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal and financial information is handled safely and responsibly, in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you apply for membership, open savings accounts, apply for loans, or use our digital platforms. This includes:

  • Identity Data (Name, Date of Birth, Civil Status, Valid IDs)
  • Contact Data (Address, Email, Phone Number)
  • Financial Data (Income, Employment Details, Assets, Transaction History)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly used for the following cooperative operations:

  • Processing membership and loan applications.
  • Managing your share capital, savings, and dividend distributions.
  • Enrolling you in our Social Services (DPK, DAP, CLP).
  • Complying with the reporting requirements of the Cooperative Development Authority (CDA).

4. Data Sharing and Protection

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with authorized third parties, such as regulatory bodies (CDA, BIR) or credit bureaus, strictly for legal compliance or credit assessment. We implement strict physical and digital security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Contact Our Data Protection Officer

If you have any questions or wish to update or delete your information, please visit our Main Office or email us at dpo@baaocoop.com.
