Humble Beginnings

Our History

It was on August 26, 1963 when the organization was first registered as Baao Parish Credit Union Incorporated (BPCUI). It started with 50 members with an accumulated Paid Capital of ₱1,193.00. Historically, the word "Parish" refers to the "Hijas de Maria", a religious organization of the Parish. From humble beginnings, it has grown into a generic powerhouse.
